Engineering

Engineering is one of the most common streams taken up by science students once they graduate from 12th.The importance of engineering can be seen on a day to day basis and the different domains of engineering widely impact in the growth of technology,specially in the 21st century.Today the most interesting and rapid growing branch is computer science but that doesnt mean other branches like civil or mechanical do not play an important part in growth of human technology.

We here are going to provide you with some of the best and top engineering exams the students can give to get into the top colleges India

  • JEE (MAIN)
  • JEE (ADVANCED)
  • BITSAT
  • MHT-CET
  • COMEDK-UGET
  • VITEEE
  • SRMJEEE
This is a list of top engineering exams in India which includes both government based and private based.Also note that to be eligible for JEE ADVANCE students first have to clear JEE MAINS.
